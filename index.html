About

Reinvent:YU is Yeshiva University's first 300+ person hackathon. For 24 Hours students team up and turn their ideas into tangible products. Build anything from Apps, Websites, Robots, to the bounds of your imagination and win prizes.

It's going to be a fantastic, inclusive environment, open to all skill levels. It's going to be amazing. Built upon our 2015 hackathon, YUHack and brought to you by YU Hackers.

Our Mission

Enable anyone with an idea the skills needed to make it reality. To bring together a community passionate about building things and making the world a better place.
